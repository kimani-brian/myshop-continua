Policies

Shipping, delivery, and returns

We keep policies transparent so you always know when your order arrives and how to reach us if something is not quite right.

Dispatch window

  • Ready-to-ship items leave the studio within 48 hours.
  • Made-to-order or monogrammed pieces ship in 5 to 7 business days.
  • You receive SMS and email tracking the moment a parcel is scanned.

Delivery coverage

  • Same-day courier within Nairobi for orders placed before 2pm.
  • Next-day line haul to Kiambu, Machakos, and Thika.
  • Twice-weekly regional routes to Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nakuru.

Need a different city? Email care@instamart.africa and we will coordinate a custom rider.

Returns & exchanges

  • Request a return within 14 days using the concierge chat.
  • Items must be unused with original packaging and tags.
  • Complimentary repairs on textiles and ceramics for six months.

Refund timeline

  • Store credit issued immediately after inspection.
  • Card refunds appear within 3 to 5 business days.
  • Mpesa refunds land same day once processed.

Need help fast?

Ping care@instamart.africa with your order number or message us on WhatsApp at +254 700 222 444. We are online Monday through Saturday.
