Our story

Grounded design for slow living

InstaMart is a Nairobi-born marketplace that curates small-batch objects, textiles, and tableware made by designers across Kenya and the region. Every capsule drop is edited by our studio team so you can build a home that feels intentional.

What guides us

  • Craft first: We visit each maker, learning their process before adding them to the roster.
  • Low impact: Packaging is plastic-free, inks are soy-based, and every shipment is offset through local agroforestry partners.
  • Radical transparency: Product pages include material provenance, care notes, and maker profiles.

Milestones

From pop-up to platform

2019

Launch in Nairobi

Started as a Saturday market residency inside a glasshouse studio featuring five independent makers.

2022

Regional fulfillment

Partnered with rider fleets and warehouse collectives to guarantee 48-hour dispatch across Kenya.

2025

Resident maker program

Launched quarterly residencies pairing ceramicists, textile artists, and perfumers for limited collaborative runs.
